Here's the steps you need to follow in order to obtain your hosting.

  1. Register your domain at a valid registrar. For example GoDaddy. When you register your domain you will be asked to set your nameservers fill them in with the servers below.

    • NS3.CRACKPOTVPS.COM

  2. Click the button below and start your PayPal subscription, billed every month at $5.00 USD per month.
  3. Send an e-mail to hosting@wowhead-tooltips.com with the following information and I will setup the account and respond with your new account information. Make sure to send the e-mail from the e-mail that you used when you started the PayPal subscription, that way I can keep things organized.

    • Desired Username Ex. crackpot
    • Desired Password
  4. I will try my best to get your account up and running as soon as I receive the e-mail from you with your desired account information. Usually within 1-2 hours after receiving the e-mail.
