Notes

  • Team record must be in XX-YY-ZZ format.
    • X equals wins
    • Y equals losses
    • Z equals overtime/shootout losses
  • Losses aren't required to calculate points, but its just to keep things clean and nicely formatted.
  • Point values
    • Wins are worth 2 points.
    • Losses are worth 0 points.
    • Overtime/Shootout losses are worth 1 point.
